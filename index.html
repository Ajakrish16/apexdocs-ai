The problem

Business teams still process critical documents manually.

Documents arrive through emails, folders, portals, scans, and PDFs. SMEs spend hours reading, comparing, correcting, and approving data before it can move to the next system.

ApexDocs.AI turns that manual process into a controlled automation workflow with human review only when needed.
